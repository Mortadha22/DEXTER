About

Chatbot technology has been around for decades, but it has recently become more popular due to advances in artificial intelligence (AI). Chatbot technology is a form of AI that enables computers to interact with humans in a natural language. Chatbots are computer programs that use natural language processing (NLP) to understand and respond to user input.
Chatbots are becoming increasingly popular as they can provide customer service, answer questions, and even provide personalized recommendations. They can be used in a variety of industries such as healthcare, finance, retail, and education.

ChatGPt allows businesses to create custom chatbots that can interact with customers in a natural way. The platform provides tools for creating conversations with customers, understanding their needs, and providing personalized responses. It also offers analytics tools that allow businesses to track customer interactions and gain insights into customer behavior.
